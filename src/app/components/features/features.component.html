Menu Management

You can easily add, edit or remove categories and items of your digital menu. Also can change the visibility of a dish, beverage or category with just one click and have an always updated menu and thus avoid the bad taste of a customer who orders elements that are not available from the menu

QR Generator

With this app you can generate a print-ready QR image for your tables, so your customers can quickly access your online digital menu.

Web menu style

Your menu will be displayed through a website that can be customized by selecting the style you most like or fits your restaurant, coffee shop or bar. All the styles are fully resposnive so they adapt to the screens of the client's devices, so any client can access and correctly display your menu on any of their devices

Manage Reservations

Get reservations from your customers in your online digital menu and manage them. You can specify the reservation configurations in the app, such as working days, max numbers of dinners, etc.
